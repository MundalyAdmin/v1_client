Welcome to Mundaly! This Privacy Policy outlines how Mundaly Enterprise ("Mundaly," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use the Mundaly platform (the "Platform"). By using the Platform, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and phone number. If you choose to participate in surveys, we may collect additional information related to your interactions with organizations.

We may collect device information, such as your device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers. Usage information, including the pages you visit within the Platform, the time and date of your visit, and other usage statistics.

To personalize your experience: We use your information to provide a more personalized and relevant experience within the Platform. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent. Your survey responses are anonymized and aggregated to provide meaningful insights to organizations.

The Platform is intended for users who are 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.
