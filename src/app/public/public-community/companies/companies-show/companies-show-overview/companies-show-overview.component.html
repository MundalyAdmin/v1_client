About

Hardworking cocoa farmers volunteer their time on a community farm to provide tuition-free education for their girls and healthcare for their families. Their motivation to work hard on the community farms is founded on the belief that it is their hard work that holds the key to educating their girls - at TBGS - for a brighter future.
