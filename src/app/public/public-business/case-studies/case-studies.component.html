Aterian plc

Project

  • Aterian in its operations in Africa has been plagued with legal issues, theft and employee turnover, disrupting their work and damaging their social reputation.

Outcome

  • We calculated the baseline social license quotients and risk in the countries they operate to prepare headline risk report, social report for FTSE Russell ESG rating and a Speakup report for responsible minerals initiative.

Mars Chocolate

Project

  • Mars gets bad PR press from a CBS investigation that reached a global audience and generated viral reaction from the general public. This gave Mars a bad reputation, their stock tank, and their sales dropped.
